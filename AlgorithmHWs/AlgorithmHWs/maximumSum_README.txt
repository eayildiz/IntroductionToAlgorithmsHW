This program finds maximum sum we can reach by adding numbers in a table. Table is nx3 table. We can select only one number from single row. Trick is, next number under the selected one, cannot be selected.

Example:
	Input:
	3
	10 40 70
	20 50 80
	30 60 90
	Output:
	210
	
	Input:
	1
	100 10 1
	Output:
	100
	
	Input:
	7
	6 7 8
	8 8 3
	2 5 2
	7 8 6
	4 6 8
	2 3 4
	7 5 1
	Output:
	46
