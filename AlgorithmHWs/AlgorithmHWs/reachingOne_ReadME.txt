This program takes four integers as input. First one is the start point and other three are cost of three process.
Process one: Substracting one.
Process two: Dividing half.
Process three: Dividing by three.

The goal is reaching one with the minimum cost.

Example:
	Input:
	180 1 1 1
	Output:
	6

	Input:
	180 1 2 3
	Output:
	14

	Input:
	180 1 6 10
	Output:
	35
