This program takes two array of integers that state ages of the people and match them based on their age preference. Maximum match is guaranteed.

Examples:
	Input:
	6 3
	10 15 20 25 30 35
	14 16 21 29 33 39
	Output:
	4
	
	Input:
	9 3
	10 15 20 25 30 35 10000 10000 10000
	3 5 6 14 16 21 29 33 39
	Output:
	4
